        Guidelines & How It Works

        Sale Rotations: A sale rotation is a group of players who take turns selling their businesses with help from others.

        • Rotations are made up of 3 to 4 players.
        • Each rotation has at least 1 seller with 1 to 6 businesses to sell.
        • Helpers with no businesses assist sellers.
        • New players can be inserted into rotations as space allows.
