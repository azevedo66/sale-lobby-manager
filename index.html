          Guidelines & How It Works

          Sale Rotations: A sale rotation is a group of players who take turns selling their businesses with help from others.

          • Rotations are made up of 3 to 4 players.
          • Each rotation has at least 1 seller with 1 to 6 businesses to sell.
          • Helpers with no businesses assist sellers.
          • New players can be inserted into rotations as space allows.

          Player Input Form:

          • Player Name: Enter the player's in game name.
          • Businesses: Enter the number of businesses the player wants to sell (0-6).
          • Mark as Non-Helper: Select this if the player is not ready to sell or doesn't want to be in a sale rotation yet. They can be entered into a rotation later when they're ready.
          • Waiting for Small Rotation: Select this if the player only wants to join smaller rotations, ensuring they're grouped more fairly.
